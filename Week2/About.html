About Vardhaman College of Engineering

Established in 1999, Vardhaman College of Engineering is located in Hyderabad, Telangana. The institute offers courses like B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech and MBA/PGDM. These courses are offered in Full Time mode. A total of 18 courses offered by the Institute are available across streams such as Engineering and Business and Management Studies. The institute has all the necessary facilities to the students. located in Shamshabad, Hyderabad, is a premier UGC-autonomous institute affiliated with JNTUH
